The Mission

You are the Spotter. Your sniper is shooting at targets. Observe where bullets hit on the reticle and give accurate correction calls to help the sniper adjust his aim.

Correction System

Corrections use directions + values:

D/U = Down/Up (vertical) | L/R = Left/Right (horizontal)

3 = Inner target zone (near center)

6 = Outer target zone (target edge)

12 = Beyond target (far off)

/ = center hit

Example: D3 R12 = 3 mils down, 12 mils right

Game Modes

Single Shot: One impact per target

Double Shot: Two impacts. Give combined correction: D3 R12; U6 L3
